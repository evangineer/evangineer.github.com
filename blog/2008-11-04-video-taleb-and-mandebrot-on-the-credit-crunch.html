Video – Taleb and Mandebrot on the Credit Crunch

Nassim Nicholas Taleb and Benoit Mandelbrot of respectively The Black Swan and Fractal Geometry fame give us a gravely pessimistic view of the financial crisis caused by the credit crunch. Taleb seems genuinely frightened, Mandelbrot is more sanguine, but also very concerned.
