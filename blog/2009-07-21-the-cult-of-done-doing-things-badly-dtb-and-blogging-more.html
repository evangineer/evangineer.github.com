The Cult of Done, Doing Things Badly (DTB) and Blogging More

I really should blog more. I have at least one blog post that I’ve been putting off because I’ve allowed myself to think I should make a real production out of it. This perfectionist streak stands in the way of getting things done and I really don’t need that. The redoubtable Vinay Gupta of Hexayurt fame often advises that if a thing is worth doing, it’s worth doing badly. Apparently, the originator of the phrase was G. K. Chesterton.

In a similar vein is the Cult of Done Manifesto. So I’m not going to get so hung up on producing polished, finished artefacts (unless someone is paying me).
