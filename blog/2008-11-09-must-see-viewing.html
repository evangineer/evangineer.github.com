Must See Viewing

Something I often do is point people to interesting and/or relevant material. A couple of recent examples that have been well received:

  • Brain Rules for Presenters is Garr Reynolds’ (of Presentation Zen fame) take on John Medina’s book Brain Rules.
  • Larry Lessig’s final Free Culture talk. The founder of the Creative Commons speaks for the last time publically on the rationale and history behind it, insightful, informative and entertaining. He also touches on his next initiative: Change Congress.
  • The Duellists where a pair of parkour practitioners almost literally run wild in the Manchester Arndale Shopping Centre. The film was shot using the in-house CCTV network of 160 cameras. QuickTime and Windows Media versions available for download. It’s a MediaShed production.
