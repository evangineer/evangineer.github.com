The Hotty Professor

Imagine The Nutty Professor meets Some Like It Hot. A nerdy male professor Barry Olson (initials BHO, same as Barack Obama a little in-joke) takes a serum of his own devising that transforms him into Ms. Betty Love, a stunning, sophisticated and graceful woman with a strong character and a rapier wit. Betty becomes best friends with one of Olson’s female students who is strangely attracted to her. Quote: “You’re everything that I look for in a man, if only you were a man!”
