Alexander The Greater

Lead character is inspired by Nick Bostrum, Stephen Wolfram and Doc Savage. Alexander Macedon was a child prodigy who went onto win a MacArthur Genius Grant for his pioneering work in combining Complexity Science with Systems Ecology. Alexander has inherited his father’s software business Corinth (logo is a stylized fist holding up a wreath of victor’s laurels) and transformed it into a globe-spanning, market-dominating corporate empire along similar lines to Apple through the creation of disruptive innovations. His family motto is Mens sana in corpore sano – a healthy mind in a healthy body. In line with this, Alexander follows a daily regimen of mind-body exercises that he learnt from his father.

Having conquered the business world, Alexander becomes more and more aware of other global concerns, particularly Existential Risks. He decides to go into the world saving business by forming Global Intervention (GI). GI is a multi-disciplinary non-profit organisation which employs a number of people. These include five of the other brightest people on the planet who work as part of an elite crisis prevention and intervention team headed by Alexander, operating initially under a UN mandate. The team take on a series of existential threats with the stakes getting higher and higher, while coming into conflict with the existing global political economy.
