Free Software for Real Brain Training

Last night, I introduced tav to Brain Workshop, it is a version of the Dual N-Back mental exercise discussed in this Wired article which has been claimed by researchers to genuinely boost intelligence.

I also showed him Mnemosyne which like SuperMemo is a flash card program which incorporates a sophisticated spaced repetition technique to optimize long term retention of the information being learned.

Incidentally, both of the above are open source software written in Python and available for Windows, Mac OS X and Linux.
